ALWAYS HERE

I won’t always have the words
to pull you back from the dark,
but I will Always be here
to sit with you in it,
and take your hand,
or wrap my arms around you
until it passes.
For every day of sunshine,
there will be a night of darkness
but I want to spend
both of them with you.

~ 05/08/2025

PERFECT

You are perfect to me.
Don’t you know?
Just like the sound of a few soft tones of laugh.
And if....
If love, had a melody
It would be in your giggle
And that look that you give.
You are,
where my heart’s been waiting to belong.
And now I know
What love is,
And I’ll dance with the starts at 3 am.
And hug the moon and hold it close.
And skip the sweet,
light fandango,
For I know
What love is.
You are perfect to me
sublime, my love.

~ 01/08/2025
